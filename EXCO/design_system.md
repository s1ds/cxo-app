Design System: CXO Knowledge ServiceAesthetic: "Executive Minimalism." High-contrast, data-dense but uncluttered. Serious, authoritative, and distraction-free.Color Palette (Dark Mode Default):Background: #0F172A (Slate 900) - Deep, professional background.Surface/Card: #1E293B (Slate 800) - Slightly lighter for content areas.Text Primary: #F8FAFC (Slate 50) - High readability.Text Secondary: #94A3B8 (Slate 400) - For metadata/labels.Accent/Action: #38BDF8 (Sky 400) - Subtle, intelligent blue for links/buttons.Success/Signal: #10B981 (Emerald 500) - For positive market signals.Border: #334155 (Slate 700) - Thin, crisp 1px borders. No soft shadows.Typography:Headings (Authority): "Inter" (Tight tracking, Bold).Body (Speed): "Inter" or "Roboto" (High legibility).Monospace (Data): "JetBrains Mono" (For financial figures or AI code snippets).UI Components:Cards: Flat, 1px border, NO drop shadows. Square corners or very slight radius (4px).Buttons: Outline style or flat solid. Text-uppercase for primary actions.Navigation: Left sidebar (Web), Bottom Tab Bar (Mobile). High contrast icons.Layout Principles:Density: High. Executives want to see 5 headlines at once, not big whitespace gaps.Grid: Strict alignment. Everything snaps to a 4px/8px grid.
